Why Glaisters Farm?

  • Log-burning stove for cosy evenings in
  • Pool table and table football in the games room
  • Bath and separate shower in the main bathroom
  • Dark skies — edge of Galloway Dark Sky Park
  • Fire pit and outdoor seating for eight
  • EV charger on site
  • All utilities, linen & towels included
  • VisitScotland 4-Star rated

Before you book

Dogs are welcome — we ask that they stay downstairs and off the furniture. The farmhouse and all outbuildings are strictly non-smoking and non-vaping.

For groups of four or fewer, an under-occupancy discount applies — check Discover Scotland for current pricing or get in touch.
